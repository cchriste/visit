3D Annotations


Figure 4

VisIt has a number of controls, located on the 3D Options tab, in the Annotation Window for controlling annotations that are used when the visualization window contains 3D plots. Like the 2D controls, these controls focus mainly on the axes that are drawn around plots. figure shows an example 3D plot with the 3D annotations labeled and figure shows the Annotation Window's 3D Options tab.
